Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

expand_more

Yes, CineStream is a monthly or yearly subscription service. You can cancel your membership online with just two clicks. There are no cancellation fees – start or stop your account anytime.

How does the yearly discount work?

expand_more

When you choose the Yearly plan, you pay for 12 months upfront at a 20% reduced rate compared to paying monthly. It’s equivalent to getting over two months free!

What devices are supported?

expand_more

Watch anywhere, anytime. Sign in with your account to watch instantly on the web at cinestream.com from your personal computer or on any internet-connected device that offers the app, including smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, and streaming media players.
